## Our Services


### Accident Assessment
We carry out a thorough inspection of a vehicle damaged in an accident, with an aim to establish the nature of impact and extend of damage to vehicle components, occasioned by the accident.

Upon inspection of the unit, we compile a comprehensive accident assessment report detailing the extent of damage, making sure to include captioned and annotated exhibit photos, and an authoritative procedure to the repairer on restoring the unit to its pre-accident condition.

In addition to outlining the damage to the vehicle, we determine the damaged parts that will need to be replaced, repaired or repainted and list them in the assessment report along with their pricing. For purposes of indemnity, we also flag parts that were damaged or defective prior to the accident, commonly known as pre-accident damages or defects. Typically, replacement or repair works to be carried out on parts bearing pre-accident defects are subject to special agreement between the insurer and the insured.

Using our experience, market knowledge and professional networks, we help in minimizing the cost of repairs by negotiating for the parts and labour prices, and identifying the most appropriate parts for vehicle model.

In the course of damage inspection and report compilation, our zero tolerance policy to dishonesty, canvassing and favouritism is ever in full force. Before submitting our reports, the management carries out proper due diligence on the outlined facts, parts recommended for replacement, their pricing among other factors to ensure that the report meets the highest standards of accountability on which we have built our business and reputation.

### Pre-Insurance Valuation
We carry out vehicle appraisal and valuations for units intended for insurance cover against risks. As independent consultants, our role is to determine the market value of the unit, to aid the insurer in computing the appropriate premium payable by the insured for the vehicle.

In the course of our valuation work, we take into account the condition of the vehicle, its odometer reading (mileage), market trends among other contributing factors to come up with an assessed market value that is reasonable and suitable for the purpose of insurance.

Our opinions are sound, data-driven and defensible, in line with our policy of fidelity to facts,
honesty and professionalism.

### Finance Collateral Valuation
Offered to banks, micro-finance companies, SACCOs and similar institutions, our finance valuation services are geared towards establishing the value of a vehicle that is meant to be used as security for a loan or credit facility.
It is imperative that our reports are firmly grounded on sound evaluation of the condition of the vehicle and prevailing market conditions to mitigate risk for the lender and the borrower alike.

### Court Bond Valuation
For clients involved in legal proceedings that necessitate the posting of a security bond, we offer vehicle valuation services for the express purpose of being posted as a court bond.

When an individual awaits trial after an arrest, they may be granted a temporary release from custody through a court bond or bail. This release is conditional upon providing the court with sufficient security, guaranteeing the accused person’s appearance at subsequent hearings. While often associated with cash payments, this security can also take the form of tangible assets like a vehicle, pledged as security.

In cases where a motor vehicle is offered as security for a court bond, a formal valuation becomes essential. We assess the vehicle’s current market value to satisfy the court that it holds adequate worth to cover the bond amount.
As a standard, we also provide our assessed forced or auction sale value for finance valuation reports to give the lender and borrower a well-rounded report for better informed decisions.

### Post-Repairs Re-Inspection
Following accident-related damage and subsequent repairs, a Post-Repairs Re-inspection report serves as a crucial verification step.

This specialized inspection is designed to confirm that the restoration work performed on the vehicle faithfully reflects the recommendations of the assessment report. It acts as an essential quality control measure, ensuring that the repairs planned and agreed upon in consultation with the claims manager, vehicle owner and repairer, have been executed correctly and completely.

Our re-inspection process involves verification of the repaired vehicle against specifications outlined in the assessment report and repairs estimate. Our experienced assessors scrutinize the work carried out, ascertaining that designated parts were indeed replaced, repaired, or repainted as specified. This includes examining the quality of workmanship, such as panel alignment and paint finish, and cross-referencing replaced components against the estimate and assessment report.

Thorough examination ensures that the repairs meet required standards and that the costs incurred correspond accurately to the work completed, providing assurance value.
