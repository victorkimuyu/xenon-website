Our Core Services

Accident Assessment

We carry out a thorough inspection of a vehicle damaged in an accident, with an aim to establish the nature of impact and extend of damage to vehicle components, occasioned by the accident.

Pre-Insurance Valuation

We carry out vehicle appraisal and valuations for units intended for insurance cover against risks.

Finance Collateral Valuation

Offered to banks, micro-finance companies, SACCOs and similar institutions, our finance valuation services are geared towards establishing the value of a vehicle that is meant to be used as security for a loan or credit facility.

Post-Repairs Re-Inspection

Following accident-related damage and subsequent repairs, a Post-Repairs Re-inspection report serves as a crucial verification step.
