About Us

A leading motor risk consultancy firm providing expert motor vehicle assessment, valuation and inspection services across Kenya.

Xenon Auto Assessors & Valuers is a leading motor risk consultancy firm, established to provide insurers, vehicle owners and organisations with accident damage assessment, valuations, mechanical inspections and appraisal services for motor vehicles. Licensed by the Insurance Regulatory Authority (IRA), our compliance and competence is fully vetted. Additionally, we are members of the Motor Assessors Association of Kenya (MAAK), the professional body that upholds best practices for industry practitioners.
