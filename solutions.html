Our Services

Accident Assessment

We carry out a thorough inspection of a vehicle damaged in an accident, with an aim to establish the nature of impact and extend of damage to vehicle components, occasioned by the accident. Upon inspection of the unit, we compile a comprehensive accident assessment report detailing the extent of damage, making sure to include captioned and annotated exhibit photos, and an authoritative procedure to the repairer on restoring the unit to its pre-accident condition.

Pre-Insurance Valuation

We carry out vehicle appraisal and valuations for units intended for insurance cover against risks. As independent consultants, our role is to determine the market value of the unit, to aid the insurer in computing the appropriate premium payable by the insured for the vehicle. In the course of our valuation work, we take into account the condition of the vehicle, its odometer reading (mileage), market trends among other contributing factors to come up with an assessed market value that is reasonable and suitable for the purpose of insurance.

Finance Collateral Valuation

Offered to banks, micro-finance companies, SACCOs and similar institutions, our finance valuation services are geared towards establishing the value of a vehicle that is meant to be used as security for a loan or credit facility. It is imperative that our reports are firmly grounded on sound evaluation of the condition of the vehicle and prevailing market conditions to mitigate risk for the lender and the borrower alike.

Court Bond Valuation

For clients involved in legal proceedings that necessitate the posting of a security bond, we offer vehicle valuation services for the express purpose of being posted as a court bond. In cases where a motor vehicle is offered as security for a court bond, a formal valuation becomes essential. We assess the vehicle’s current market value to satisfy the court that it holds adequate worth to cover the bond amount.

Post-Repairs Re-Inspection

Following accident-related damage and subsequent repairs, a Post-Repairs Re-inspection report serves as a crucial verification step. This specialized inspection is designed to confirm that the restoration work performed on the vehicle faithfully reflects the recommendations of the assessment report. It acts as an essential quality control measure, ensuring that the repairs planned and agreed upon have been executed correctly and completely.
